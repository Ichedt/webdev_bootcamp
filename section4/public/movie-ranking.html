Oscars 2024 Movies Ranked

My ranking of the movies indicated to Best Picture in the Oscars 2024.

10. Maestro

Just taking a place because it was indicated. Does not reserve much.

9. The Holdovers

Good movie taking us back to the 80's.

8. Past Lives

A "sit back and appreciate" experience.

7. American Fiction

Good acid humour and satires about our world and our hypocrisies.

6. Barbie

The movie about the iconic doll, with some critiques about our foundations.

5. The Zone of Interest

Intense movie with excellent use of the sound to portray the holocaust in an different way.

4. Killers of the Flower Moon

Excellent movie about the atrocities commited against the Osage people.

3. Oppenheimer

The best biography I've ever seen. With the Nolan's touch.

2. Anatomy of a Fall

Sublime. An excellent exercise on our judments and how we see strong women.

1. Poor Things

The best. Everything in this movie is perfect. Emma Stone's best performance and with no doubt one of the best movies ever made.
